### Optimal parameters of indoor environment

<span style="display:none"></span> | CIS | EU
-- | -- | --
temperature | 20-25 °C | 20-26 °C
humidity | 30-60 % | 25-60 %
co2 | < 1000 ppm | < 900 ppm
